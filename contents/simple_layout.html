About HR-P

What is HR-P

HR-P is PaaS for HRM. Now a days, HR department of business uses variety of ICT systems and Web services. The cooperation of these systems would be very complex with the growth of a company. On the other hand, for strategic HR business, ICT systems has to be strong to change. HR-P will provide a flexible and integrated HRM systems easily.
